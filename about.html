Frequently Asked Questions

ADU eligibility depends on property size, zoning requirements, setbacks, utility access, HOA guidelines, and local development standards. Property evaluations can help homeowners understand opportunities available throughout Dallas, DFW, and surrounding North Texas communities.

Detached ADUs, backyard cottages, garage conversion apartments, guest houses, and in-law suites remain among the most requested residential projects. Many Dallas homeowners explore these options to create additional living space and increase long-term property functionality.

Accessory dwelling units can provide flexible living arrangements for family members, guest accommodations, rental income opportunities, home offices, and multigenerational housing while helping homeowners make better use of available property space.

Most accessory dwelling unit projects require permits, plan reviews, and approvals before construction can begin. Requirements may vary based on property conditions, project scope, zoning regulations, HOA requirements, and local development standards throughout Dallas and surrounding DFW communities.

Many homeowners consider garage conversion ADUs because they can create additional living space without expanding the main residence. Property value benefits may vary depending on location, design quality, functionality, market conditions, and overall development goals.

Our contractor network supports detached ADUs, garage conversions, and guest house projects throughout Dallas, DFW, and North Texas with planning, permitting, and construction coordination.
